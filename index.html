Learn about one of these three cool animals!

Snake!

Snakes are cold-blooded or exothermic animals which means they get their body heat fron the sun rather than generating it internally.

Octopus!

Octopuses are cephalods, a class of intelligent molluscs distantly related to slugs & snails.

Red Panda!

Red panda's are endangered relatives raccoons that are found exclusively in China.
